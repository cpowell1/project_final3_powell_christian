About Peyton's Place

Peyton's Place is a dog boarding and daycare service in Nashville, TN. Peyton's Place was started to give dog owners a piece of mind that their best friends would be cared for when you are away. The owner, Christian Powell, created Peyton's Place after her own dog, Peyton. This red labrador retriever brought the most joy Christian had felt in her life, and she realizes how important these best friends are to their owners.

Here are Peyton's Place, we don't cut corners. We strive to provide your dog with all of their needs because we know they are pretty much humans. From long nature walks to one-on-one cuddle sessions, your dog will have quality care. We know we cannot replace your dog's love for you, but we sure know how to love on your dog while you are away!
