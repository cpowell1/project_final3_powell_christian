Grooming

The bathery was created with a focus on the wellbeing of pets in every aspect. After extensive research, the Salon was equipped with a Hydro-Surge massaging bath system, and then stocked with Aroma Paws shampoos and conditioners. These products are all-natural and oatmeal-based, which means that they are gentle on even the most sensitive skin. They come in a variety of natural scents—or fragrance-free. Our goal is to create what we call “Stress-Free Bathing.”

Training

There are many reasons to take your dog through Lifestyles Obedience classes, ranging from simple day-to-day good behavior to taking the first step toward becoming a Therapy Dog. Whether you have a new puppy, or a mature dog, you can learn how to establish a working relationship with your dog and how to achieve your goals.

Peyton's Place is working with longtime Nashville dog trainer, Donald Trump to bring you an engaging, intuitive and effective six-week course with two different levels: Basic and Intermediate.
